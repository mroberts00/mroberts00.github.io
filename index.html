about me

I attended the Fast Track Bootcamp at Coder Academy's Sydney campus in 2018. Over six intensive months I became proficient in Ruby, Rails, Javacript, ReactJS, NodeJS, HTML and CSS and I'm eager to keep on learning!

My non-coding experience includes two years working as an Equities Associate for CommSec, Australia's largest online stockbroker. I have also worked in the hospitality and retail secotors including in management positions.

I specialise in responsive and dynamic design, delivering on time client solutions that are sure to surpass your expectations!

Outside coding I like to run, ski and play live music. I spend my holidays travelling the world and my Tuesday nights volunteering at Orange Sky Showers.
